Transpaer

Transpaer is a project aimed to help people make better decisions as customers and help them find alternatives to the products they buy that are more aligned with their values. In other words, makes the consumer market more transparent. All in one place, accessible quickly and easily, e.g. with a barcode scan with your phone.

Go to the web app Download Android app (comming soon)

Transpaer is a community project. Anyone with enough skills and determination can joint it, share ideas, propose new features and contribute.

For consumers

Buying products from companies aligned with your values and boycotting those that are not aligned is the most effective way of influencing the world around us. Especially it we do it all together.

Maybe you don't like the lost of wild life habitats, climate change or other human-made impacts on the nature. Maybe you dislike billonair CEOs and venture capitalists and would like to support small, local business. Maybe you want to eat more healthy. Maybe you want to take no part in violent conflicts by refusing to buy products benfiting aggresor countries. Maybe you want to make sure all products you buy are made without animal suffering. Maybe you want to help your country to break from dependance on other countries by supporting it's own economy. Transpaer can help you achieve those goals.

For companies

Transpaer is still a very small community, but when it grows it will be full of people determined to vake sure their maoney go to the companies aligned with their values. If you share those values Transpaer can become the easier place to find more customers. Let us know about your new sustainable product, your XXX policies or other things you are proud of.

For experts

Research about market and impact of products is typically not very accessible for regular consummers. If you are an expert working in consumer market research Traspaer can make your work more available to cosumers and thus more impactful.

Contact

The best way to contact us is via the our Discord server. There we can discuss your ideas and proposals. That the prefered way of contact even for companies. At Transpaer we want to be as transparent as possible about all decisions.
